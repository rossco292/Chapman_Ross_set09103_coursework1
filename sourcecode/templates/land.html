Land

Land can be thought of as mana/cost as it is what is used to play any cards you have. Now land in and of itself is a card and there is a rule that you can only play one land a turn. This rule can be ignored by some card effects. When you play a land it stays on the field and to use a land you need to tap it and once a land is tapped you cant use it for "mana" again till your next turn.
